Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing guide

How to Measure

For accurate measurements, use a soft measuring tape and follow these guidelines:

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping one finger between the tape and your body.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.
